Pricing FAQs

Are there any hidden fees? ▼
No. The price you see is the price you pay. Installation labor, hardware, configuration, and training are all included. The only optional add-on is 24/7 professional monitoring at $29/month.
Can I upgrade my system later? ▼
Yes! You can add devices and features anytime. Your installer can expand your system as your needs grow. There's no contract locking you into anything.
What's included in the installation fee? ▼
Installation includes: hardware purchase, professional setup, Z-Wave/Zigbee hub configuration, device pairing, dashboard customization, scene setup, training, and full handoff. Everything needed to go from zero to fully operational.
What about device costs beyond the quoted hardware? ▼
The quoted price includes hardware. If you want to expand beyond the base package, your installer will quote additional devices. You control what gets installed and when.
Is monitoring really optional? ▼
100% optional. You can use EJG-OS without any monitoring subscription. Add it later if you want professional 24/7 response for security alerts. Cancel anytime.
