The Software That Sets You Apart

A Platform Built for Professional Installers

EJG-OS isn't just a smart home OS — it's a professional-grade installer toolkit. Every feature was designed to save you time on site, eliminate repeat work, and make your installs look flawless every single time.

📚

Device Libraries

Every device you've ever paired is catalogued in your personal device library. Thermostats, locks, cameras, bulbs, sensors — each one saved with its manufacturer, model number, protocol type, and optimal configuration settings. When you pull a device out of the box on a new job, it already knows what it is and how to set it up.

What this means on site: No more looking up pairing instructions. No more configuring the same Schlage lock settings for the 30th time. The library remembers everything so you don't have to.
🎬

Scene Libraries

Scenes are what make a smart home feel magical — and your scene library is where that magic is stored. Good Morning, Good Night, Movie Mode, Away, Welcome Home — build them once, save them to your library, and deploy them across every client home with one click. Clients see a polished product from the first power-on.

Pro tip: Create client-specific scenes on site and they get saved to the library automatically. Over time, your scene library becomes your competitive advantage — no other installer has your collection.
💾

Memorized Templates

This is where the real time savings happen. Memorized templates let you save an entire home configuration — every device, every room assignment, every scene, every automation rule — as a single reusable template. Building the same model of home for a developer? Apply the template. Client wants the exact setup their neighbor got? Apply the template. Done.

Templates include device placement, room names, automation triggers, scene configurations, and even the dashboard layout. What used to take 3 hours of on-site configuration now takes minutes.

Automation Templates

Automations are the backbone of a great smart home experience — but building them from scratch every time is tedious. EJG-OS gives you a library of pre-built automation templates that cover the most common scenarios, and you can create and save your own.

Time-based, motion-based, temperature-based, sunrise/sunset, geofence — every trigger type is supported. Combine triggers with conditions and actions in a visual builder that takes seconds, not minutes. And every automation runs 100% locally, so there's zero cloud latency.

The installer dashboard is organized into three distinct layers, each built for a different level of work. You can zoom in and out between them seamlessly.

See every home you've ever installed or are currently managing. Health status indicators, device counts, last service date, client info — all in one scrollable list. This is your bird's-eye view.
