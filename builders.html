The Process

From Framing to Front Door

We fit seamlessly into your construction schedule. Here's how a typical builder project runs.

1

Builder Consult

We meet with your team to understand your floor plan, specs, and buyer expectations. We provide a pre-wire guide for your electrical sub.

2

Pre-Wire Stage

Your electrician follows our spec sheets during rough-in. No special expertise required — we provide detailed zone maps for every device.

3

Device Installation

Our certified team installs all smart devices after drywall and paint. Thermostats, switches, locks, cameras — all go in cleanly and professionally.

4

System Commissioning

We configure the EJG-OS hub for the home. Scenes, automations, and device names are set up based on the buyer's pre-selected preferences.

5

Buyer Handoff

We walk the new homeowner through their system at possession. App installed, scenes tested, questions answered. They move in confident.
