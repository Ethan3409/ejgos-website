EJG-OS Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

EJG Technologies ("we", "us", or "our") operates the EJG-OS application. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

The EJG-OS app collects and stores the following on your device only:

  • The local IP address of your EJG-OS hub (entered by the installer during setup)
  • App configuration preferences stored locally on your device

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. All communication is between your tablet and your local EJG-OS hub on your home network.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Android device using Android's SharedPreferences system. No data is sent to EJG Technologies or any third party.

Network Access

The app connects only to your EJG-OS hub on your local home network (or via your Tailscale VPN if configured). It does not connect to any external servers operated by EJG Technologies.

Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — to connect to your local EJG-OS hub
  • WAKE_LOCK — to keep the screen on for wall panel use
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — to auto-launch after device restart
  • RECORD_AUDIO — for the optional Skip voice assistant feature (microphone access)

Children's Privacy

EJG-OS is intended for homeowners and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL.
